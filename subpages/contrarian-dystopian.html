Contrarian-Dystopian

Examples of Contrarian-Dystopian Design

Simultaneity: The human body portrayed in various ways, not in any specific category or for the purpose of delivering a message

Constraints Project: Unconventionally wooden package used to deliver snacks with cuss words

Carnivorous Plants: Models of plants in a mechanical form, misleading or delivering no information to the audience

Pillow-Thoughts: Modeled personal space with smoke/thought bubbles that could be misunderstood as encouraging harmful habbits
